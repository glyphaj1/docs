<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="What is haunt.gg?">
**haunt.gg** is a biolink platform that allows you to create customizable profiles with features such as **social links, effects, themes, and more**.  
It’s designed to help you showcase your online presence effectively.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is haunt.gg free?">
Yes — haunt.gg is completely **free** to use. You can create and customize your profile without any cost or hidden fees.  
We also offer a [**Premium plan**](../guides/premium) with additional features for those who want more customization. **95%** of the platform remains free.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Where can I buy Premium or other products?">
You can purchase all products on our [**Pricing page**](https://haunt.gg/pricing).  
You can also **gift products** to other users, which will earn you the [**Gifter badge**](https://help.haunt.gg/customization/badges#gifter-badge).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Which payment methods are accepted?">
- **Stripe**  
  - Credit/Debit Card, Google Pay, EPS-Transfer (Bank Transfer), Klarna, Apple Pay, Giropay  
- **Cryptocurrencies (via Cryptomus)**  
  - USDT, BTC, LTC, ETH, XMR, POL, BNB, USDC, SOL  
- **PayPal**  
  - Processed through our trusted Point of Sale software, Paddle
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is multiaccounting allowed?">
Yes, multiaccounting is allowed with certain limitations. [**Read more**](../guides/multiaccounting).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is imagehost free?">
Yes, our **image host** is free to use.  
You can increase storage limits by purchasing [**Premium**](https://haunt.gg/pricing) or the [**Image Host upgrade**](https://haunt.gg/pricing).  
View all limits [**here**](https://help.haunt.gg/guides/imagehost#limits).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How to get 1 or 2 Letter usernames?">
- **1-letter (1L)** usernames: `50€`  
- **2-letter (2L)** usernames: Included with [**Verified or Premium**](https://haunt.gg/pricing)  

To purchase a **1L username**, contact us via [**Discord**](https://discord.gg/hauntbio).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I claim banned usernames?">
Yes — if you are a **Premium user**, you can claim banned usernames.  
To claim one, open a ticket in our [**Discord server**](https://discord.gg/hauntbio).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I buy banned UIDs?">
Yes, banned UIDs can be purchased:  

| UIDs   | Price |
| ------ | ----- |
| 0–10   | 50€   |
| 10–50  | 25€   |
| 50+    | 10€   |

Contact us via [**Discord**](https://discord.gg/hauntbio) to purchase.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I apply for a verified badge?">
Yes. Read the [**requirements**](../guides/verification#requirements).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How to get the OG badge?">
The **OG badge** is awarded to users who have been on haunt.gg since the beginning.  
It **cannot** be obtained by new users.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What badges are on haunt.gg?">
You can view all badges on our [**Badges page**](../customization/badges#badges).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I apply for staff?">
Check our [**Apply page**](https://haunt.gg/dashboard/apply).  
If an application for a **Staff position** is open, you can apply.  
If there’s no application listed, staff applications are currently closed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I secure my haunt.gg account?">
Read our [**Security Guide**](../guides/security) to learn how to protect your account.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I report bugs or suggest features?">
You can report bugs or suggest features in our [**Discord server**](https://discord.gg/hauntbio) or via the [**Apply Page**](https://haunt.gg/dashboard/apply).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I request deletion of my data?">
Yes — email [**support@haunt.gg**](mailto:support@haunt.gg) from the same email address linked to your account.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you offer regional pricing?">
We completely understand that our prices may seem excessive in some areas.  
We would love to provide **regional pricing**, but some people would take advantage of it in order to save money.  
We appreciate your understanding that **prices are the same for everyone**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does haunt.gg have an API?">
Yes. Our API lets you fetch **user** and **leaderboard** data. [**Read more**](https://help.haunt.gg/endpoint/introduction).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I report content on haunt.gg?">
Yes. If you find content that violates our [**Terms of Service**](https://haunt.gg/terms), you can report it:  

**Profiles**  
- Right-click anywhere on a user's profile.  

**Imagehost uploads**  
- Click the flag icon when viewing the image.  

**Assets/Templates**  
- Report via ticket in our [**Discord server**](https://discord.gg/hauntbio).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you have an affiliate program?">
Yes. [**Read more**](../guides/affiliate).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What characters are allowed in my username?">
You can use all characters in the English alphabet, dots(periods), underscores, and hyphens.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is selling accounts allowed?">
Selling accounts or UIDs is not allowed.  
However, selling usernames is permitted.
</Accordion>


<Accordion title="How do I delete my haunt.gg account?">
Follow the steps in our [Account Deletion Guide](../guides/account-deletion).
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
