        - Account Ban
        - Account warning
        - Application Review

        - Includes automation to sort files into folders by filename


    **Themes & Layouts**
    - Added new **Portfolio Theme**
        - Create projects with **title, description, tags, link & icon**
        - Choose from different **skills** to showcase what you can do
        - Supports [**About Me**](../customization/customization#about-me) feature to show **important information** about yourself
        - Supports [**Social Cards**](../customization/themes#supported-social-integrations), similar to the **Modern theme**
    
    **UI/UX Improvements**
    - Removed social card limitation which prevented you from adding multiple profiles per social app
    - Added **Secondary Text Color** customization to change the color of the small text in presence cards or audio control
    - Added **Badge Unified** which gives the ability to reorder badges without restrictions
    - Added a customization option for the size of the **profile, audio player & volume control**
    - Added support for custom emojis in the **About Me** section

    **Visual & Decorative Updates** 
    - Added **VHC Bodycam** background effect
    - Added **new decorations** from Discord

    **Terms of Service Updates**
    - Updated **Multiaccounting policy**
        - It's allowed to have multiple accounts with limitations.
        - The profiles must be active, like having customizations, links, etc.
        - You can have up to 3 accounts. This limit can be increased to 5 accounts by having premium on your main account.
        - We reserve the right to ban accounts that do not meet the requirements.
          
</Update>
