## Participation Rules
    To ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for all participants in haunt.gg events, please adhere to the following rules:
    ### 1. **Originality**
        > All submissions must be original works created by the participant. Plagiarism or copying of others' designs is strictly prohibited.
    ### 2. **Theme Adherence**
        > Submissions must align with the specific theme or guidelines provided for each event. Designs that do not follow the theme may be disqualified.
    ### 3. **Submission Deadline**
        > All entries must be submitted before the specified deadline. 
    ### 4. **One Entry Per Participant**
        > Each participant is allowed to submit only one entry per event unless otherwise specified.
    ### 5. **Respectful Content**
        > All designs must be appropriate and respectful. Offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content will lead to disqualification.
    ### 6. **Judging Criteria**
        > Submissions will be judged based on creativity, adherence to the theme, and overall design quality.
    ### 7. **Disqualification**
        > Failure to comply with any of the above rules may result in disqualification from the event.

## Voting Rules

These rules are for the voting phase after finalists have been selected:

    ### 1. **One Vote Per Person**
        > Each individual is allowed to cast only one vote per event.
    ### 2. **Fair Voting**
        > Participants are encouraged to vote based on the quality and creativity of the submissions, rather than personal relationships.
    ### 3. **No Vote Manipulation**
        > Any attempts to manipulate the voting process, such as creating multiple accounts to vote, will result in disqualification.
    ### 4. **No Vote Solicitation**
        > Participants may not ask others to vote for them.

        This includes:
        - Direct requests in comments or messages (e.g., Discord, haunt.gg, social media, etc.)
        - Descriptions or statuses on social media (Discord, haunt.gg, other bio links, etc.)
        - Any other form of solicitation
    ### 5. **No Incentivized Voting**
        > Participants may not promise or offer rewards in exchange for votes.
