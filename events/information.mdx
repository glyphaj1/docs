## What are haunt.gg events?
**Haunt.gg Events** are special activities or competitions organized by **haunt.gg** to engage and inspire the community.
These events usually feature specific themes, encouraging participants to redesign their profiles based on given categories. Participants can submit their designs for a chance to win exciting rewards.

## What are the rewards for participating in haunt.gg events?
Usually the prices are the following:
  - **1st Place:** <Badge color="yellow" icon="trophy">Winner</Badge> Badge + `25€` of haunt.gg products in value.
  - **2nd Place:** <Badge color="gray" icon="medal">Second Place</Badge> Badge + `10€` of haunt.gg products in value.
  - **3rd Place:** <Badge color="orange" icon="medal">Third Place</Badge> Badge + `5€` of haunt.gg products in value.

<Info>
  Prizes can vary depending on the event. Please refer to the specific event announcement for detailed information about the rewards.
</Info>
