To participate in events, firstly create your event themed profile. Our [**Customization Guide**](../customization/customization) provides detailed instructions on how to customize your profile.

<Warning>
    __Make sure to follow all the guidelines and rules specified for the event before participating.__.
</Warning>

After you have created your themed profile, submit it through the ``#event`` channel in our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/hauntbio).
Your message has to include:
  - A link to your haunt.gg profile.
  - A screenshot of your profile.
