## How can I customize my profile?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Customize Page">
    Visit [haunt.gg/dashboard/customize](https://haunt.gg/dashboard/customize) to get started.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assets Uploader

To upload your assets, follow the instructions [here](../customization/assets).

## General Customization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Section">
    Go to the **General Customization** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Customizing">
    You’ll find various options to personalize your profile, like adding a description or applying effects.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  ![General Customization Interface](../images/general-customization.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
To set an enter text, you need to have an audio or video selected.
</Warning>

---

## Color Customization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Section">
    Go to the **Color Customization** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Colors">
    Here, you can change colors for icons, text, and more.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  ![Color Customization Interface](../images/color-customization.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
You can’t choose an accent color if a profile gradient is active.
</Warning>

---

## Border Customization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Section">
    Go to the **Border Customization** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust the Border">
    You can enable/disable the border and adjust its radius here.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  ![Border Customization Interface](../images/border-customization.png)
</Frame>

## Glow Customization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Section">
    Go to the **Glow Customization** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the Glow">
    Customize the glow strength for text, icons, and badges.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  ![Glow Customization Interface](../images/glow-customization.png)
</Frame>

---

## Presence Customization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Section">
    Go to the **Presence Customization** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust Presence">
    Toggle visibility for different presence options like decorations, status, tags, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Showcase Your Discord Guild">
    Enable this setting to display your Discord guild or community on your profile.

    This is a great way to highlight the group you're part of—especially useful for teams, clans, or shared communities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  ![Presence Customization Interface](../images/presence-customization.png)
</Frame>

---

## About Me

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Section">
    Go to the **About Me** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit">
    Create a personalized About Me for your profile using supported Markdown.

    <Frame>
    ![About Me Customization Interface](../images/about-me-01.png)
    </Frame>

    <Tip>
    You can also run the `/emoji` command in our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/hauntbio) to get a markdown code for a custom emoji to use in your "About Me".
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable About Me">
    Turn on the About Me section by toggling the switch in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  ![About Me Example](../images/about-me-02.png)
</Frame>

---

## Advanced presence settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate Advanced Presence Settings">
    Enable advanced presence settings by toggling the option on in the **Presence Customization** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Configure your advanced Discord presence settings however you like.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  ![Discord Presence Settings](../images/advanced-presence.png)
</Frame>

---

## Other Customization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Section">
    Go to the **Other Customization** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="More Options">
    Set features like volume control, the parallax effect, and others.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  ![Other Customization Interface](../images/other-customization.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
To use volume control, a video or audio must be set.
</Warning>

<Note>
The parallax effect creates subtle movement in the background when the mouse moves.
</Note>
