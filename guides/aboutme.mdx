Your **About Me** section is often the first thing people read on your profile. A well-formatted bio makes your profile readable, professional, and visually appealing. Overly long text, weird spacing, or too many emojis can make it look messy.

Here’s how to make your About Me section clean, attractive, and easy to read.

## Basic Text Formatting

You can make your text stand out using:

* **Bold:** `**Your text**` → **Your text**
* *Italic:* `*Your text*` → *Your text*
* __Underline:__ `__Your text__` → __Your text__
* ~~Strikethrough:~~ `~~Your text~~` → ~~Your text~~

### Quick Tips

* Avoid using all caps for long sections — it’s hard to read.
* Keep sentences short and break text into paragraphs.

---

## Headers and Alignment

Headers help break up your text and guide readers.

* `#` → Header 1  
* `##` → Header 2  
* `###` → Header 3  

You can also align text:

* `<left>Your text</left>`  
* `<center>Your text</center>`  
* `<right>Your text</right>`

<Note>
Proper alignment makes your bio easier to scan, especially for stats or socials.
</Note>

---

## Links

Links can make your bio interactive.

* **Links:** `[text](url)` → [Haunt.gg](https://haunt.gg)  

### Quick Tips

* Make sure links formatted correctly.

## Final Advice

* Keep your bio **short and structured**. People skim profiles.  
* Experiment with different formatting styles.

<Tip>
A clean, well-formatted About Me section keeps your profile looking professional and tidy.
</Tip>
