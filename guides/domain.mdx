## Official Domains

- These are the official domains that are available and ready to use: [haunt.gg](https://haunt.gg/), [exitscam.xyz](https://exitscam.xyz/), [fentanyl.ing](https://fentanyl.ing), [rich.bz](https://rich.bz/), [haunt.bio](https://haunt.bio/), [best-bio.site](https://best-bio.site)

### How can I use those domains?

- You just need to choose your preferred domain name and add your username after the slash (Example: [https://exitscam.xyz/bka](https://exitscam.xyz/bka)). Then, you can add it to anything you want, like your Discord description!
