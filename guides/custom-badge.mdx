## How to Buy a Custom Badge on haunt.gg

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Purchase Page">
    Go to [haunt.gg/pricing](https://haunt.gg/pricing) and scroll down until you see the "Get Custom Badge" section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Buy Button">
    Click on the "Get Custom Badge" button to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Payment Method">
    Select your preferred payment option and complete the purchase.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Custom Badge Features

<Note>
Display any image as a badge on your profile with a custom name. Update your badge image or name anytime.
</Note>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Badge Appearance">
    - Upload any image to showcase in your **Badge section**.
    - Add a **custom name** to personalize your badge.
    <Frame>
      ![Custom Badge Preview](../images/custom-badge-preview.png)
    </Frame>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Badge Editor">
    - Edit your badge image or name anytime from the socials > badges > my badges section.
    - Preview changes before saving them.
    <Frame>
      ![Custom Badge Editor](../images/custom-badge-editor.png)
    </Frame>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Display on Profile">
    - Your custom badge appears alongside other badges on your profile.
    - Hover over the badge to see the custom name you set.
    <Frame>
      ![Badge On Profile](../images/custom-badge-on-profile.png)
    </Frame>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Unique Identity">
    - Combine your custom badge with other features to stand out.
    - Perfect for creators, fans, or anyone wanting a unique badge.
    <Frame>
      ![Badge Unique Identity](../images/custom-badge-identity.png)
    </Frame>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
